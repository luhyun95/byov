The Cost Breakdown of Villa Construction in Bali

Building your own villa in Bali is a dream come true for many. However, understanding the costs involved is crucial for turning that dream into a reality. In this article, we'll break down the general costs of villa construction in Bali, giving you a clear picture of what to expect.

1. Construction Costs

The largest portion of your villa budget will likely go into construction. On average, villa construction in Bali costs approximately 5 million to 10 million Indonesian Rupiah per square meter. Factors such as the choice of vendors and the quality of materials can influence this range. Don't forget to budget separately for a swimming pool if you desire one.

2. Architect and Interior Design

While architectural and interior design services are optional, they can greatly enhance the aesthetics and functionality of your villa. Architect and interior design costs typically range from 350,000 to 1,000,000 Indonesian Rupiah per square meter, depending on the designer's expertise and reputation.

3. Furnishing

Furnishing your villa is the final touch that brings it to life. As a general rule of thumb, budget around 11% of your construction cost for furnishing. This includes furniture, decor, and any other items you need to make your villa comfortable and inviting.

4. Additional Considerations

Keep in mind that there may be additional costs such as permits, taxes, and utility connections. It's essential to factor in these expenses to avoid surprises along the way.
